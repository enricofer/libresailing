Sailing at large

Originally when I purchased FSM, my plan was to sail the Mediterranean and just beyond for the first few years, until I had full knowledge and confidence in the boat and of all its components. The time has come to widen FSM's horizon.
My initial idea was to start from the Red Sea and head towards Asia, but the ongoing tensions in the area changed my plans there. Instead, I opted for a "classic" tour, towards the west, following the trade winds.
As usual, I will be happy to share some section of the tour with those who are willing to engage in my idea, which is essentially based on sustainability, both ecological and economic, on minimalism and simplicity, and on sharing.

La route

This is the general program:

  • Spain - Canary Islands
    • distance: 700 NM
    • minimum duration of sailing: 7 days
    • variation: Morocco (+150 NM)
  • Canary Islands - Martinique
    • distance: 2700 NM
    • minimum duration of sailing: 24 days
    • variations: Senegal/Gambia (+800 NM), Cabo Verde (+100 NM), Guyane (+500 NM)
  • Martinique - Panama
    • distance: 1200 NM
    • minimum duration of sailing: 12 days
    • variations: Guadeloupe, Cuba (+400 NM), San Blas
